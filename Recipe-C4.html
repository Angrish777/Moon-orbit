Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg with milk and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a skillet and pour in batter to form pancakes.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.

Enjoy your homemade pancakes!
